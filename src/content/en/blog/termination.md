## **1. Legal Basis, Notice Period, and Termination Timeline**

Pursuant to the SORA Card Terms and the applicable Unlimit card program rules:

- The SORA Card service may be **terminated with notice**, or **with immediate effect where required for regulatory, compliance, or operational reasons**.
- During the wind-down phase, **certain services, features, or accounts may be restricted, suspended, or disabled earlier** than the final termination date.

## **3. Issuer-Level Account Suspensions by Unlimit**

Please note the following important distinction:

- **Some SORA Card accounts are currently suspended directly by the issuer, Unlimit**, under its regulatory, compliance, and risk management obligations.
- These suspensions are **issuer-level actions**, outside the operational control of SORA Card, SORA Biome, or application partners.
- Suspended accounts may still show balances but remain **temporarily inaccessible** pending issuer review or release.

### **SORA Biome Actions**

**SORA Biome is actively engaging with Unlimit** at program level to **push for the release of balances** on suspended accounts wherever legally and operationally possible.

However:

- **Only Unlimit has the authority to release funds or lift suspensions**
- Users must therefore **act directly with Unlimit** to request access to any remaining balances

## **4. User Guide: How to Request Release of Funds from Unlimit**

If your account is suspended or shows a remaining balance after the app cut-off, please follow these steps:

### **Step-by-Step Guide**

1. **Prepare Your Details**
    - Full legal name
    - Email address registered with SORA Card
    - Cardholder ID or last 4 digits of your card (if available)
    - Screenshot or record of remaining balance (if accessible before cut-off)
2. **Contact Unlimit Directly**
    - Use Unlimit‚Äôs official support or compliance contact channels
    - State clearly that you are a **SORA Card cardholder requesting release of remaining funds**
3. **Request Explicitly**
    - Ask for:
        - The **reason for the suspension**, and
        - The **procedure to withdraw or release your remaining balance**
4. **Complete Compliance Requests**
    - Unlimit may request additional KYC or documentation
    - Prompt responses will help avoid delays
5. **Escalate if Needed**
    - Request escalation to Unlimit‚Äôs **compliance or card program team** if no response is received within a reasonable timeframe
    - Retain copies of all correspondence
